Everything you need to know about using TikTok Toolkit

🔒 Privacy First

This tool operates entirely in your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers, ensuring your privacy and security. All processing happens locally on your device.

Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. We never store your URLs, downloaded content, or personal information on our servers. Everything stays on your device and is completely private.
Our tool respects TikTok's rate limits to ensure reliable service. For normal usage, you shouldn't encounter any restrictions. If you're downloading many videos, we recommend spacing out your requests to avoid temporary blocks.
We provide the highest quality available from TikTok, typically up to 720p HD. Video quality depends on the original upload quality. Audio downloads are provided in high-quality MP3 and M4A formats.
We strive to provide watermark-free downloads when possible. However, this depends on TikTok's current API and policies. Some videos may retain watermarks due to platform restrictions.
Your last 5 checked URLs are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is only used to help you quickly access recently checked videos. You can clear this history anytime.
Some videos may be restricted due to privacy settings, geographic restrictions, or platform policies. Try checking if the video is public, or wait a few minutes and try again. Private accounts and region-locked content cannot be accessed.
We support MP4 for video downloads (best compatibility), MP3 for standard audio, and M4A for high-quality audio. All formats work across different devices and platforms.
